Artist Statement

For me, I see Art in everyday living. It comes in a spiritual form that evokes emotions and inspires us. My Art speaks for itself based on how you can relate to it. Making Art is some sort of liberation for me, a form of solace where freedom is set. My Art enlightens more on Social issues, so in this case I use abstract to illustrate where and how the society is channeling towards the future. I always choose this approach towards my Artworks and it stands out and making sure I am portraying the right message to Art lovers and the masses. I can say my Art is more futuristic because I believe in the future and evolution. People often tell me how my art is different and how it predicts the future and what the world awaits. KWAKU DARTEH is a digital artist Born and raised in the capital city of Accra, Ghana. He developed his interest for art after high school when he discovered Microsoft paint. His interest got higher after graduating with diploma in graphics. He tells real life stories through his art. He looks forward to change the world for good through his art and inspire generations to come. The world motivates him and he motivates the world.
